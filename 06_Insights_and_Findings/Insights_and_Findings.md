# Insights and Findings

This section summarises key analytical insights derived from the Power BI dashboard and warehouse model.

---

## 1️⃣ Market Activity Overview

- Total transactions exceed 27,000 across the observed period.
- Median prices remain consistently high, indicating a strong and stable property market.
- Premium segment represents a smaller share (~7%), suggesting mid-market dominance.
- Total sales value exceeds $28B, reflecting substantial capital turnover.

Key Insight:
The market demonstrates strong liquidity with consistent pricing performance, supported primarily by mid-segment transactions.

---

## 2️⃣ Price Trends Over Time

- Median price shows cyclical monthly movement.
- Notable dip observed mid-period followed by recovery.
- Transactions volume increases toward late 2017.

Key Insight:
Price correction phases appear temporary, with resilience in overall market value. Transaction activity may lead price movement cycles.

---

## 3️⃣ Regional Performance

- Southern Metropolitan region shows the highest median price (~$1.58M).
- Northern Metropolitan records the highest transaction volume.
- Eastern Metropolitan maintains strong price levels with moderate transaction count.

Key Insight:
Premium pricing does not necessarily correspond to highest transaction volume, indicating distinct buyer segments across regions.

---

## 4️⃣ Suburb-Level Analysis

- Top-performing suburbs exceed $2M median price.
- Price dispersion across suburbs is significant.
- High-value suburbs cluster within metropolitan zones.

Key Insight:
Location remains the dominant price determinant, reinforcing spatial concentration of property value.

---

## 5️⃣ Spatial Distribution Patterns

- Geographic clustering is evident around central and premium regions.
- Higher transaction densities align with metropolitan accessibility.
- Peripheral regions show lower price and transaction intensity.

Key Insight:
Urban proximity and infrastructure accessibility strongly influence transaction density and pricing concentration.

---

## 6️⃣ Strategic Implications

- Mid-price band drives overall market stability.
- Premium segment remains niche but high-value.
- Regional differentiation supports targeted investment strategies.
- Spatial concentration suggests continued metropolitan demand resilience.
